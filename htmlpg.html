Metaphor

A metaphor is a mapping between one domain to another. For example a moo is to cow and a beep is to car.These mappings gets so burried in our language that we dont even notice their effects.

For example take a language used to debate:
    "He shot down her ideas"
    "She deferated his arguements"
    "He had to retreat from that positions"

These are all debate at war metaphors. So the idea where both the people could lern something each other doesnt come-up.
